Sydney Pugh

PhD Candidate at the University of Pennsylvania

I am a graduate student at the University of Pennsylvania pursuing my PhD in Computer and Information Science. My advisors are Insup Lee and James Weimer. My research interests include machine learning, Internet of Medical Things (IoMT), and security. I earned a M.S. in Computer and Information Science from the University of Pennsylvania in 2021 and a B.S. in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science from Loyola University Maryland in 2019. I am open to internship and research fellowship opportunities in the Philadelphia and DMV area.
