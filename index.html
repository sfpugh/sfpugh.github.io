Bio

I am a second-year PhD student in the Department of Computer and Information Science at the University of Pennsylvania. My advisors are professor Insup Lee and professor James Weimer. My research interests include the Internet of Medical Things (IoMT), security and privacy, medical cyber-physical-systems (CPS), and security of CPS.
I earned my Bachelor's of Science degree in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science at Loyola University Maryland in 2019 under advisors professor David Binkley and professor Lisa Oberbroeckling.
